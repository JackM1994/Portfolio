About this site

This webpage was created to help first year students understand the basics of Spanish. The webpage is simple to use with tabs displayed on the left hand side of the screen. Each tab brings the student to a new learning section. The sections include colours, numbers, days and months with the last section displaying flip cards the student can interact with. The flip cards act as study notes, with the questions being constantly updated to help continous learning.
